Root canal treatment is used to save a tooth when the pulp inside becomes infected or inflamed.

At Lumina Dental Care, the process usually includes:

1. Digital imaging and diagnosis
2. Local anesthesia for comfort
3. Cleaning and sealing the root canals
4. Final restoration with a filling or crown

Most patients return to normal routines quickly. Mild sensitivity for a short period is expected, and your doctor will guide you on after-care and follow-up.
