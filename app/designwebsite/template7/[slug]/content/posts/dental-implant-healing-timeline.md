Dental implant recovery is usually smooth when post-op instructions are followed carefully.

Typical timeline:

1. **Days 1-3:** Mild swelling and tenderness are common
2. **Week 1:** Soft diet and gentle cleaning around the area
3. **Weeks 2-6:** Gum healing improves and discomfort reduces
4. **2-4 Months:** Bone integrates with the implant (osseointegration)
5. **Final Stage:** Crown placement once stability is confirmed

To support faster recovery, avoid smoking, maintain oral hygiene, and attend review appointments on schedule.

If you notice persistent pain, unusual swelling, or bleeding, contact your dentist immediately.
