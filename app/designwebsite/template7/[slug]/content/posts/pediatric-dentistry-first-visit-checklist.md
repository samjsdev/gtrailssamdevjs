---

The first dental visit sets the tone for your child's long-term oral health habits.

Before the appointment:

1. Schedule a morning slot when your child is rested
2. Explain the visit positively using simple words
3. Carry previous records and current medication details
4. Avoid promising sweets as a reward

During the visit, dentists usually assess tooth development, gum health, and brushing habits. Parents also receive preventive guidance on diet, fluoride, and cavity risk.

Regular checkups every 6 months help identify issues early and reduce the need for complex treatment later.
