STUDIO MANUALS

Design & Sizing Advice

Expert advice on dimension safety, layout routing, and material longevity.

Budget Planning

How to Set a Realistic Interior Design Budget

A good budget is not just a number. It is a set of priorities that tells the project where to spend, where to save, and which decisions matter most for your lifestyle.

Key Tip

“List must-haves separately from nice-to-haves”

Space Planning

Why Your Furniture Layout Matters More Than Decor

Decor can make a room beautiful, but layout makes it livable. The right furniture placement improves movement, comfort, storage access, and the way people naturally gather in a room.

Key Tip

“Measure door swings, window positions, and passage widths”

Lighting

The Three Lighting Layers Every Home Needs

Lighting is one of the fastest ways to make a space feel expensive, calm, and functional. A single ceiling light rarely does enough. Great rooms use layers.

Key Tip

“Avoid relying on one bright ceiling fixture”

Materials

How to Choose Finishes That Age Well

Materials should look beautiful on day one and still make sense years later. The best finish palette balances durability, maintenance, texture, and the amount of natural light in your home.

Key Tip

“View samples in your actual room light before approval”

Kitchen Design

What Makes a Modular Kitchen Easy to Use

A kitchen works best when storage, appliances, counters, lighting, and movement are planned together. Beauty matters, but daily rhythm matters more.

Key Tip

“Keep daily utensils near the hob and prep counter”

Execution

How to Prepare Your Home Before Site Work Begins

A smooth site phase starts before the first worker arrives. Clear decisions, protected belongings, access planning, and communication rhythm can save time and stress.

Key Tip

“Clear rooms before carpentry or civil work starts”

Common Questions

What happens during the first design consultation?+

We discuss your lifestyle, rooms, budget range, timeline, inspiration references, and functional pain points. From there, we outline a design direction and the next steps for measurements, layouts, and material planning.

Can you work with my existing furniture or finishes?+

Yes. We can design around pieces you want to keep, refresh existing rooms, or create a phased plan that mixes new furniture, custom storage, lighting, and decor with your current home.

Do you handle execution as well as design?+

Yes, projects can be handled as design-only or end-to-end execution. For turnkey work, we coordinate vendors, drawings, site updates, material selections, and handover quality checks.

How do you keep the project on budget?+

We define priorities early, separate must-haves from nice-to-haves, and align every major material, furniture, and execution choice to the agreed budget before site work begins.
